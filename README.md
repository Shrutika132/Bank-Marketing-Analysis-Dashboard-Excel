📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a bank marketing dataset using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides insights into customer behavior, job distribution, education levels, and campaign outcomes. It helps identify which customer segments are more likely to respond positively to marketing campaigns.

---

🎯 Key Features

👤 Customer Segmentation – Analysis based on marital status (Single, Married, Divorced).

💼 Job Analysis – Distribution of customers across different job roles.

🎓 Education Insights – Combined analysis of job roles and education levels.

📊 Outcome Analysis – Campaign success, failure, and other outcomes.

📈 Interactive Dashboard – Easy-to-use filters and visuals in Excel.

---

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

🔹Microsoft Excel – Data analysis and dashboard creation.

🔹Charts & Graphs – Visualization (Bar, Pie, Column charts).

🔹Data Cleaning – Preprocessing and transformation.

---

📊 Dashboard Insights

🔹Majority of customers belong to management and technician roles.

🔹Campaign success rate is relatively lower compared to failures.

🔹Education level influences customer response to campaigns.

🔹Married customers form a significant portion of the dataset.

---

✅ Results

🔹Identified target customer segments for marketing campaigns.

🔹Improved understanding of customer demographics.

🔹Enabled better decision-making for future marketing strategies.
